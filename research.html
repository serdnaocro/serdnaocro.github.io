Research

I'm currently interested in music cognition and the computational modeling of music perception, especially as they pertain to rhythm and meter — our ability to infer and abstract meter and temporal structure from music. In previous research, I focused on rhythmic and harmonic complexity in contemporary jazz.

Current threads

Rhythm & meter cognition

How listeners infer and abstract meter and temporal structure from music, and how that can be modeled computationally.

Embodied cognition

Music perception and performance as grounded in the body, not only in abstract symbol-processing.

Improvisation & agency

The cognitive processes of improvisation — in particular, the role of non-conscious processes in consciousness-driven agency in music.

Musical time

Notions of time across physics, mathematics, and philosophy, and their relationship to musical time.
